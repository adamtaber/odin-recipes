Hot Pocket

Description

A hot pocket is a microwavable calzone-like item. It has a seasoned bready exterior with a melty, cheesy, meaty interior.

Ingredients

  • Box of hot pockets

Steps

  1. Open box of hot pockets
  2. Take out one hot pocket and open the wrapper
  3. Place the hot pocket in the crisping sleeve
  4. Place on a plate and put in the microwave for 2 minutes
